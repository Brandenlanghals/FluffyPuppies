Frequently Asked Questions

If your question is not listed, please feel free to call us at 216.555.1234


  1. What do you charge for full grooming?

    2. All of our prices are based on breed, size of your pet, and the condition of their coat. When you make an appointment, we are able to give you a quote of what your pet's grooming price will be.
  2. How far in advance should I schedule an appointment?

    3. We are typically booked out several weeks in advance, please plan accordingly.
  3. Do you groom cats?

    4. Yes! We have cat groomers at both salon locations.
  4. Do you have any breed or size restrictions?

    5. We groom all breeds and sizes of both dogs and cats.
  5. How often should my pet be groomed?

    6. We recommend all pets be groomed every 6 weeks or sooner. Your groomer can set up a grooming schedule for your pet to help keep them clean and tangle free.
  6. Do you allow tipping?

    7. Tipping is never required, but always appreciated.
